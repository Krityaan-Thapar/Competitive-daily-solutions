# Question 

_Difficulty: Easy_

You are given a natural number ‘N’. Find all the divisors of the number ‘N’. Print them in increasing order.
Example:
Input: ‘N’ = 10

Output: [1, 2, 5, 10]
1, 2, 5, and 10 are the only divisors of the number 10.

**Examples :**
```
Sample Input 1 :
2
100
13

Sample Output 1 :
1 2 4 5 10 20 25 50 100
1 13

Explanation Of Sample Input 1 :
For the first case:
100 is divisible by 1 2 4 5 10 20 25 50 100 only.
For the second case:
13 is divisible by 1 and 13 only.

Sample Input 2 :
2
125
15

Sample Output 2 :
1 5 25 125 
1 3 5 15
```

**Constraints:**
```
1 <= T <= 10
1 <= N <= 10^9

Time Limit: 1 sec
```
